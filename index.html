About zonne.ai

zonne.ai was born from a vision to transcend traditional property listings. Melding drone technology with AI, we paint dynamic, interactive portraits of homes. Each model uncovers layers of detail that static images or flat videos can’t capture.

We focus on minimalism, authenticity, and empowerment...

At zonne.ai, we harness advanced drone technology and proprietary AI to deliver immersive 3D property models that highlight what matters most: location. By capturing every angle from the sky, we seamlessly stitch together breathtaking point clouds that showcase each home’s layout and its surrounding area.

Our dedication to a premium and luxury-appealing design aesthetic ensures that both real estate professionals and prospective buyers can experience properties in a visually striking, intuitive interface. This emphasis on high-end presentation differentiates listings and conveys the true essence of each space.

As a growing startup, we blend recurring SaaS revenue models with an innovative approach to drone flights, 3D rendering, and AI-driven property insights. Our goal is to lock in partnerships and build a scalable, future-forward platform that continuously adapts to the evolving needs of real estate professionals worldwide.
