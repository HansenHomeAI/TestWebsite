About ⁶space.ai

Real estate is all about location—yet most listings fail to showcase it properly. Photos and 3D tours capture the inside of a home well, but they leave out what matters most: the context, the surroundings, the feel of the space.

That’s where ⁶space.ai comes in.

How it works.

Plan your flight – A drone pilot generates a flight path right here on our platform.

Capture and upload – The drone follows the path, taking photos, which are then uploaded back to us.

Get your model – We process the images, and within 3 days, you receive a fully immersive 3D model straight to your inbox.

We make it effortless to turn aerial drone images into stunning, interactive 3D models—so buyers don’t just see a property, they experience it. No expensive equipment, no complicated software. Just clarity.

Bringing AI to real estate.

Noticing firsthand how listings fail to capture what buyers actually care about, 20-year-old founder Gabriel Hansen set out to fix it. Teaching himself computer science from the ground up, he built ⁶space.ai—combining advances in neural networks with drone technology to create a whole new way to showcase real estate location. The name references 6 dimensions of movement through space (a term common among AR/VR communities), and pronounced sick-space it reflects exactly what it is: a sick, radical new way to immerse yourself in a location.

Our mission is simple: Make the best tool for property marketing.
